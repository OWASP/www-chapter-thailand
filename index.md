## Welcome
Welcome to OWASP Thailand Chapter page.

## Participation
The Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) is a nonprofit foundation that works to improve the security of software. All of our projects ,tools, documents, forums, and chapters are free and open to anyone interested in improving application security. 

Chapters are led by local leaders in accordance with the [Chapter Leader Handbook](/www-policy/rules-of-procedure/chapter-handbook). Financial contributions should only be made online using the authorized online donation button. To be a SPEAKER at ANY OWASP Chapter in the world simply review the [speaker agreement](/www-policy/speaker-agreement) and then contact the local chapter leader with details of what OWASP Project, independent research, or related software security topic you would like to present.

In Thailand, OWASP documents including guidelines and tools have, in the past, been translated and released by volunteers, including but not limited to the current core members of OWASP Thailand. These guidelines and tools have been utilized by government-related organizations.

OWASP Thailand Chapter was established and announced in the Thai's Government Gazette on 25 October 2018 as "Open Web Application Security Association - Thailand." ("สมาคมความมั่นคงปลอดภัยด้านแอปพลิเคชัน - ภาคพื้นประเทศไทย") that can operate in accordance with the objectives of the Council announced in the Association's regulations document. 
[Thai's Government Gazette](http://www.ratchakitcha.soc.go.th/DATA/PDF/2561/D/081/T_0045.PDF)

## Objective

- Raise awareness on application security for members and personnel involved in the software industry
- Promote, develop, and increase the skills of members of the association In security, applications And information technology systems to be modernized to be accepted both within and outside the country
- Create knowledge and information for those interested in application security
- Transfer of professional experience in information technology security. Including the promotion and support of research for international career advancement
- Suggestions and recommendations for the development of appropriate and safe applications For the country's economic and social development To relevant agencies, both government and private sectors continuously
